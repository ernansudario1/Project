Table Arrangement

Isinulat ni staz

Table Arrangement. Made in 2017 my very first artwork.

When I was in Highschool (Grade 10) I was inspired by how a hotel table arrangement are.

Why there is a flower? a fruit, and a wine?

Then I was amaze of how was just it looks like so I drew it. So how come I drew it?

I was bored for the whole week vacation because Christmass is coming and then I just remembered the table arrangement. It was just like what our table was decorated. So do I drew just like how the typical Hotel table arrangement are, and to fullfill my boredom I paint it for 3 days.

Painting is not that easy for me because it was my very first painting.

I commit erasures and errors but I hide it as you can see the fruit colored with red and the other of it is Green. Too many acrylic paint was used specially the red one but its all worth it.

For 3 days I drew that painting I realized how a masterpiece must treasured.

It was made because of my imagination and inspiration its an art!

So value your talent and make it grow because you dont know it can be useful in the future. Materials used: acrylic paint
